Accessible Features Mobility-friendly amenities
- - i Accessible Features (8 criteria)

Scored out of 8 features, converted to 0-5 scale:
1. Step-free internal access OR lift*
2. Downstairs bedroom
3. Downstairs bathroom/WC
4. Ground floor entry
5. Off-street/private parking
6. Garden access
7. Balcony/terrace/patio
8. External level/ramp access

*Multi-level properties score for having a lift/stairlift. Single-level properties automatically score for step-free access.

i How Overall Score Works

Features are combined to produce an overall score ranging from 0 to 5.

Only information within the property listing is used for scoring. Where information is missing, it is advised to ASK AGENT.

Where information is unavailable, that feature will not score and will not affect the overall score. This ensures properties aren't penalised for missing data.
